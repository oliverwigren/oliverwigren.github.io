About

I started out as an indie game developer at 16, shipping games to the App Store, Google Play, and Microsoft Store — learning to build, market, and maintain real products from scratch.

Today I work as a junior assistant at Vinge, one of Sweden's leading law firms, where I'm part of their Legal Tech Lab — exploring how AI can reshape the legal industry. I'm also a McKinsey Forward Program alumnus.

In between I've studied economics at Uppsala University, earned a CS50 and Front-End Engineer certification, and picked up a hackathon win along the way.
